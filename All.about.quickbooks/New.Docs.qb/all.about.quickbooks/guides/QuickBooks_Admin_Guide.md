- **Required Fields**: Legal name, address, phone, email, business type

- **Industry Selection**: Choose from predefined industry categories
- **Business Structure**: Corporation, LLC, partnership, sole proprietorship

## User Management & Permissions

### User Account Management

#### Creating User Accounts
- **Navigation**: Settings → Users & Roles → Add User
- **User Types**: Regular user, accountant user, company administrator
- **Contact Information**: Email, phone, emergency contact
- **Login Credentials**: Username, password requirements
- **Profile Settings**: Time zone, date format, language preferences

#### Role-Based Access Control
- **Predefined Roles**:
