## Step-by-Step Payment Setup

### Method 1: Credit Card Payment

#### Step 1: Access Payment Settings
1. Log in to QuickBooks Online
2. Click on the **Gear icon** (⚙️) in the top right
3. Select **Account and Settings**
4. Click on the **Billing & Subscription** tab
5. Click **Update payment method**

#### Step 2: Add Credit Card
1. Click **Add payment method**
2. Select **Credit Card** as payment type
3. Enter card details:
   - Card number (16 digits)
   - Expiration date (MM/YY)
   - CVV security code (3-4 digits)
   - Cardholder name
4. Enter billing address:
   - Street address
   - City, State, ZIP code
   - Country
5. Click **Save**

#### Step 3: Set as Default Payment
1. Return to payment methods list
2. Click the **star icon** next to your card
3. Confirm as default payment method

### Method 2: Bank Account (ACH) Payment

#### Step 1: Navigate to Bank Setup
1. Go to **Gear icon** > **Account and Settings**
2. Select **Billing & Subscription**
3. Click **Add payment method**
4. Select **Bank Account**

#### Step 2: Enter Bank Information
1. Enter account details:
   - Account holder name
   - Bank name
   - Routing number (9 digits)
   - Account number
   - Account type (Checking/Savings)
2. Enter billing address
3. Click **Connect Bank Account**

#### Step 3: Verify Bank Account
1. QuickBooks will initiate micro-deposits
2. Check your bank statement for two small deposits
3. Return to QuickBooks and enter the exact amounts
4. Click **Verify** to complete setup

### Method 3: PayPal Payment

#### Step 1: Link PayPal Account
1. Go to **Billing & Subscription** settings
2. Click **Add payment method**
3. Select **PayPal**
4. Click **Link PayPal Account**

#### Step 2: Authorize Connection
1. Log in to your PayPal account
2. Review permissions requested
3. Click **Agree and Connect**
4. Return to QuickBooks to confirm

### Method 4: Wire Transfer (Enterprise)

#### Step 1: Request Wire Instructions
1. Contact QuickBooks Sales team
2. Request wire transfer payment option
3. Receive wire instructions and invoice

#### Step 2: Process Wire Transfer
1. Use provided bank details
2. Include invoice number in reference
3. Process payment through your bank
4. Email confirmation to QuickBooks billing team

## Billing Cycle Management

### Understanding Billing Cycles

#### Monthly Billing
- **Billing Date**: Same date each month
- **Payment Due**: Immediately upon billing
- **Grace Period**: 15 days before service suspension
- **Proration**: Daily proration for plan changes

#### Annual Billing
- **Billing Date**: Anniversary of signup date
- **Payment Due**: 30 days before renewal
- **Discount**: 20% savings vs monthly
- **Cancellation**: 30-day notice required

### Managing Billing Cycles

#### View Current Billing Information
1. Go to **Gear icon** > **Account and Settings**
2. Select **Billing & Subscription**
3. Review current plan and billing cycle
4. Check next billing date and amount

#### Change Billing Cycle
1. Click **Change billing cycle**
2. Select new cycle (Monthly ↔ Annual)
3. Review prorated charges
4. Confirm change and process payment

#### Update Billing Address
1. Navigate to billing settings
2. Click **Edit** next to billing address
3. Update address information
4. Save changes

## Subscription Management

### Upgrading Your Plan

#### Step-by-Step Upgrade Process
1. **Access Plan Settings**
   - Go to Gear icon > Account and Settings
   - Select Billing & Subscription
   - Click "Upgrade Plan"

2. **Select New Plan**
   - Review available plans
   - Compare features and pricing
   - Select desired plan tier

3. **Review Changes**
   - See prorated charges
   - Review new features
   - Check user limits

4. **Process Payment**
   - Enter payment information
   - Confirm upgrade
   - Receive confirmation email

#### Upgrade Considerations
- **Immediate Access**: New features available instantly
- **Prorated Billing**: Pay only for remaining billing cycle
- **User Migration**: Existing users automatically upgraded
- **Data Preservation**: All data remains intact

### Downgrading Your Plan

#### Downgrade Process
1. **Initiate Downgrade**
   - Go to Billing & Subscription
   - Click "Change Plan"
   - Select lower-tier plan

2. **Review Impact**
   - See features that will be lost
   - Check user limit reductions
   - Review billing changes

3. **Schedule Downgrade**
   - Choose effective date
   - Confirm downgrade
   - Receive confirmation

#### Downgrade Timeline
- **Monthly Plans**: Effective next billing cycle
- **Annual Plans**: Effective at renewal date
- **Immediate Changes**: Some features removed immediately
- **Data Retention**: All historical data preserved

### Adding/Removing Users

#### Add New Users
1. **Access User Management**
   - Go to Gear icon > Manage Users
   - Click "Add User"

2. **Set User Details**
   - Enter user email and name
   - Select user role
   - Set access permissions

3. **Send Invitation**
   - QuickBooks sends email invitation
   - User accepts and sets password
   - Billing updated automatically

#### Remove Users
1. **Select User**
   - Go to Manage Users
   - Find user to remove

2. **Remove Access**
   - Click "Remove" or "Make Inactive"
   - Confirm removal
   - Billing adjusted automatically

## Troubleshooting Payment Issues

### Common Payment Problems

#### Declined Credit Card
**Symptoms**: Payment fails, error message displayed
**Solutions**:
1. Check card expiration date
2. Verify sufficient funds
3. Contact card issuer
4. Try alternative payment method

#### Bank Account Verification Issues
**Symptoms**: Micro-deposits not received or incorrect
**Solutions**:
1. Wait 1-2 business days for deposits
2. Check all bank accounts for small deposits
3. Enter exact amounts (including cents)
4. Contact bank if deposits not received

#### Billing Address Mismatch
**Symptoms**: Payment declined due to address verification
**Solutions**:
1. Update billing address in payment settings
2. Match address exactly with card statement
3. Contact card issuer to update records

### Failed Payment Recovery

#### Automatic Retry Process
1. **First Failure**: Automatic retry in 24 hours
2. **Second Failure**: Retry in 3 days
3. **Third Failure**: Account suspension warning
4. **Final Failure**: Service suspension

#### Manual Payment Processing
1. **Update Payment Method**
   - Add new payment method
   - Remove failed payment method

2. **Process Manual Payment**
   - Contact QuickBooks billing support
   - Provide alternative payment details
   - Service restored within 24 hours

### Service Suspension Process

#### Suspension Warning
- **Email Notification**: 7 days before suspension
- **Portal Alert**: Red warning banner in QuickBooks
- **Grace Period**: 15 days to resolve payment

#### During Suspension
- **Read-Only Access**: Can view but not edit data
- **Payment Portal**: Still accessible for payment
- **Support Access**: Limited support during suspension

#### Service Restoration
1. **Process Payment**: Pay outstanding amount
2. **Verification**: QuickBooks verifies payment
3. **Service Restore**: Full access restored within 1 hour
4. **Confirmation**: Email confirmation sent

## Cancellation and Refunds

### Cancellation Process

#### Voluntary Cancellation
1. **Access Account Settings**
   - Go to Gear icon > Account and Settings
   - Select Billing & Subscription

2. **Initiate Cancellation**
   - Click "Cancel subscription"
   - Select cancellation reason

3. **Review Cancellation Terms**
   - See final billing amount
   - Review data retention policy
   - Confirm cancellation

4. **Process Final Payment**
   - Pay any outstanding charges
   - Receive cancellation confirmation

#### Cancellation Timeline
- **Monthly Plans**: Effective immediately
- **Annual Plans**: Effective at period end
- **Data Retention**: 1 year after cancellation
- **Re-activation**: Available within 60 days

### Refund Policy

#### Eligible Refunds
- **Trial Period**: Full refund within 30 days
- **Billing Errors**: Full refund for overcharges
- **Service Issues**: Partial refunds for outages
- **Cancellation**: No refunds for unused service

#### Refund Processing
1. **Submit Refund Request**
   - Contact QuickBooks billing support
   - Provide account and invoice details

2. **Refund Review**
   - QuickBooks reviews request
   - Approval process: 3-5 business days

3. **Refund Issuance**
   - Original payment method
   - Processing time: 5-10 business days
   - Email confirmation provided

### Data Export After Cancellation
