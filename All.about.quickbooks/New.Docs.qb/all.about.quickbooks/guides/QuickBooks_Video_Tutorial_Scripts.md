## Production Guidelines

### Video Production Standards
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (1080p) minimum
- **Frame Rate**: 30 fps for smooth motion
- **Audio Quality**: Clear voiceover with minimal background noise
- **Graphics**: Professional on-screen text and callouts
- **Branding**: Consistent QuickBooks branding and colors

### Content Delivery Best Practices
- **Pacing**: Allow 3-5 seconds for users to absorb complex information
- **Demonstrations**: Show real QuickBooks interface, not mockups
- **Voiceover**: Professional, clear, and engaging narration
- **Transitions**: Smooth transitions between topics
- **CTAs**: Include calls-to-action for further learning

### Accessibility Requirements
- **Closed Captions**: Accurate captions for all spoken content
- **Audio Descriptions**: Descriptions of visual elements
- **Keyboard Navigation**: Support for keyboard-only users
- **Color Contrast**: High contrast for visual accessibility
- **Multiple Formats**: Provide transcripts and downloadable guides
