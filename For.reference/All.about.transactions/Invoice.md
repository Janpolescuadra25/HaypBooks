How Haypbooks Handles Online Payments
- Setup & Integration
- You must sign up for Haypbooks Payments and connect it to your Haypbooks account.
- Once enabled, payment options (cards, ACH, PayPal, Venmo) appear on invoices and sales receipts.
- Customer Payment Process
- When you send an invoice, the customer sees a “Pay Online” button.
- They can pay instantly using the enabled methods (Visa, MasterCard, AmEx, Discover, ACH bank transfer).
- Payments are processed through Haypbooks’ secure portal.
- Deposit Handling
- Haypbooks batches all payments received in a day into a single deposit.
- Each deposit shows details: number of transactions, fees, payer info, and payment method.
- Funds typically take 1–3 business days to reach your bank account, though instant deposits are available for a fee.
- Bank Feed Matching
- When deposits hit your bank account, Haypbooks automatically matches them to invoices or sales receipts.
- If auto‑matching fails, you can manually select “Match” to link the payment to the correct transaction.
- Flexibility
- You can also accept payments in person (card reader), via mobile app, or by sending one‑time payment links.
- For recurring billing, Haypbooks supports AutoPay, where customers authorize recurring charges.

- Create Invoice → Enable “Pay Online” → Customer pays via card/ACH → Haypbooks batches deposit → Deposit appears in bank feed → Auto‑matched to invoice → Books updated automatically.

- Faster payments: Customers pay directly from the invoice.
- Automation: Deposits and matches reduce manual bookkeeping.
- Flexibility: Supports online, mobile, and recurring payments.
