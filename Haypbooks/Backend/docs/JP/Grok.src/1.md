### Freemium Strategy for Practice Hub (Inspired by Xero)

Xero does this brilliantly: they give a **generous free trial** (30 days full access), then tiered plans where basic is limited, and advanced features (multi-currency, projects, expenses claims) are locked behind higher tiers. Users feel the value first, then pay to keep it.

For **HaypBooks Practice Hub**, we can do something similar but **more gamified and firm-friendly** — let users unlock advanced tools for free through onboarding actions (proving they're a real firm), or pay to skip straight to Pro.

#### Core Model: "Earn or Pay to Unlock"
- **Free Forever Tier** (Basic Practice Management)
  - Client list + invitations
  - Basic tasks & assignments
  - Team roles (up to 3 members)
  - Messages (internal only)
  - Goal: Attract real firms, filter out abusers.

- **Advanced Tools** (locked initially)
  - Workflow Board + Reviews Queue
  - Firm Insights & Reports
  - Firm Books (your own accounting)
  - Advanced messaging (client threads, templates)
  - Onboarding pipelines, SOP templates

#### Sequence to Unlock Advanced Tools for Free
Make it a **guided "Practice Setup Path"** — users complete steps to prove commitment and unlock features progressively. This educates them on best practices while gating power tools.

1. **Step 1: Add Your Team** (unlock basic team features)
   - Invite/add at least 2 team members.
   - Reward: Unlock full team management + basic assignments.

2. **Step 2: Onboard Your First Clients** (unlock client tools)
   - Send 3+ invitations or add 3 active clients.
   - Reward: Unlock client health tracking + Needs Attention tab.

3. **Step 3: Create Real Work** (unlock execution)
   - Create 10 tasks + assign/review 5.
   - Reward: Unlock Workflow Board + Reviews Queue.

4. **Step 4: Run a Month-End** (unlock insights)
   - Complete one full month workflow (tasks, reviews).
   - Reward: Unlock Firm Insights + basic Reports.

5. **Step 5: Manage Firm Books** (unlock Pro core)
   - Enter basic firm expenses/revenue.
   - Reward: Unlock full Firm Books + advanced messaging.

**Completion Bonus**: Finish all steps → full Pro access free for 6 months (or forever with limits).

#### Pay to Skip (Premium Shortcut)
- **"Go Pro Now" button** on every locked feature.
- Pricing:
  - $49/month (or $39 annual) → instant full Pro unlock.
  - Marketing: "Skip the setup and get advanced tools today — perfect for busy practices."

#### Why This Works (Better Than Xero)
- **Xero**: 30-day trial → pay or lose everything (high churn risk).
- **Your model**: Earn free access by using the product (proves value + filters non-serious users).
- **Psychological win**: Users feel they've "earned" Pro — higher retention.
- **Revenue**: Serious firms pay to skip (time = money).
- **Anti-abuse**: Steps require real activity (invites, tasks) — fake firms drop off.

#### Visuals in App
- Progress bar in sidebar: "Unlock Pro: 3/5 steps complete".
- Locked features: Teaser card with "Complete setup to unlock" or "Go Pro Now".
