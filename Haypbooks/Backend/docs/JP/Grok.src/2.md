### How to Read This Flowchart
- **Start at the top**: Everyone gets free basic access.
- **Two paths**:
  - **Left (Earn Free)**: Complete real actions (prove you're a serious firm) → gradually unlock everything, end with free Pro (limited time).
  - **Right (Pay)**: Instant Pro — perfect for busy firms.
- **Goal**: Users feel progress ("I'm earning this") while you filter abusers and monetize time-savvy users.
