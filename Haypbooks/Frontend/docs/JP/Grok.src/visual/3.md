Yes, that's exactly right — **Xero Practice Manager (XPM)** is **not free at the start** (you get a short trial, then pay if you're new/bronze). It becomes **free forever** *only if you reach and maintain* Silver, Gold, or Platinum status.

### How to Make XPM Free Forever
You earn **partner points** through activities like:
- Adding clients to paid Xero subscriptions (biggest source — points based on plan size and billing).
- Completing Xero certifications/training (for you/staff).
- Other actions (e.g., attending events, using certain features).

Points requirements (approximate, can vary slightly by region):
- **Bronze/New**: 0–74 points → XPM paid after trial.
- **Silver**: 75+ points + at least 1 certified staff → XPM **free**.
- **Gold/Platinum**: Higher points (300+) → XPM free + extra perks.

Status is checked regularly (quarterly or monthly). As long as you **maintain** the points and certification, XPM stays free forever.

### What If All Clients Leave Your Practice?
- Your points drop (since most come from client subscriptions).
- If points fall below Silver threshold → You **lose free XPM**.
- Xero will notify you, and you'll need to:
  - Start paying for XPM (or downgrade/cancel).
  - Or regain points by adding new clients/certifications to climb back to Silver.

It's not "forever free no matter what" — it's tied to your ongoing partner status/activity.
