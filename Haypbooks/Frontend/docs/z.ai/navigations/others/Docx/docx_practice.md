PART 1: PRACTICE HUB OVERVIEW
The Practice Hub is designed for accounting firms, bookkeeping practices, and financial advisors who manage multiple Business Owner Hub clients. It provides a centralized workspace to access, manage, and serve clients efficiently.
1.1 Key Features
Feature	Description	Scope
Client Access	Access all Owner Hub clients from a single dashboard with full transparency	Multi-client
Multi-Client Management	Switch between clients seamlessly without separate logins	All clients
Work Management	Track tasks, deadlines, and deliverables across all clients	Real-time
Books Review	Review and reconcile client books with audit trail	Per client
Client Portal	Branded client portal for communication and document exchange	On-demand
Practice Analytics	Insights into practice performance, utilization, and profitability	Real-time
Team Management	Manage staff, assign work, and track team performance	Ongoing
Billing & Pricing	Flexible billing methods, rate cards, and client invoicing	Per engagement
Workflow Templates	Standardized processes for recurring services	Reusable
Compliance Tracking	Monitor filing deadlines and compliance across clients	Multi-jurisdiction

1.2 User Roles & Permissions
Role	Description	Key Permissions
Partner	Firm owner or partner with full access	All clients, billing, team management, practice settings
Manager	Senior staff managing client engagements	Assigned clients, team oversight, billing approval
Senior Accountant	Experienced staff handling complex work	Assigned clients, time entry, work completion
Staff Accountant	Entry to mid-level staff	Assigned tasks, time entry, client access as assigned
Admin	Administrative support	Client onboarding, scheduling, document management
Contractor	External resource with limited access	Specific clients/tasks, time entry only

PART 2: QUICK SETUP (Initial Onboarding)
Quick Setup for Practice Hub configures your firm account and prepares you to start serving clients in 15-25 minutes.
2.1 Quick Setup Steps

Step 3: Services Offered	Bookkeeping, Tax Prep, Audit, Advisory, Payroll, Consulting	Enables relevant modules
