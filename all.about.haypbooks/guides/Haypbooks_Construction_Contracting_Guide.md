## Construction Industry Overview

### Construction Business Models

#### General Contracting
- **Residential Construction**: Single-family homes, multi-family units, renovations
- **Commercial Construction**: Office buildings, retail spaces, industrial facilities
- **Institutional Construction**: Schools, hospitals, government buildings
- **Infrastructure Construction**: Roads, bridges, utilities, public works

#### Specialty Contracting
- **Electrical Contracting**: Electrical systems, wiring, lighting, power distribution
- **Plumbing Contracting**: Plumbing systems, water supply, drainage, HVAC
- **Mechanical Contracting**: HVAC systems, refrigeration, process piping
- **Roofing Contracting**: Roofing installation, repair, waterproofing

#### Construction Services
- **Design-Build Services**: Integrated design and construction services
- **Construction Management**: Project management and coordination services
- **Renovation and Remodeling**: Existing building renovation and upgrade
- **Maintenance Services**: Facility maintenance and repair services

### Key Construction Metrics

#### Financial Metrics
- **Gross Margin**: Revenue minus direct costs divided by revenue
- **Overhead Rate**: Indirect costs as a percentage of direct costs
- **Labor Productivity**: Revenue per labor hour or per employee
- **Material Cost Control**: Material costs as a percentage of total costs

#### Operational Metrics
- **Project Completion Rate**: Projects completed on time and within budget
- **Change Order Frequency**: Change orders as a percentage of contract value
- **Subcontractor Performance**: Subcontractor on-time delivery and quality ratings
- **Safety Incident Rate**: Workplace safety incidents per 100,000 hours worked

### Construction Industry Challenges

#### Project Management
- **Schedule Management**: Complex project scheduling and coordination
- **Resource Allocation**: Labor, equipment, and material resource management
- **Quality Control**: Construction quality assurance and inspection
- **Client Communication**: Regular progress updates and expectation management

#### Financial Management
- **Cost Control**: Real-time cost tracking and budget management
- **Cash Flow**: Managing cash flow across multiple projects
- **Profitability**: Ensuring profitability on individual projects
- **Tax Compliance**: Construction-specific tax requirements and deductions

#### Regulatory Compliance
- **Licensing Requirements**: Contractor licensing and certification
- **Building Codes**: Local building code compliance and permits
- **Environmental Regulations**: Environmental impact and waste management
- **Safety Standards**: OSHA compliance and workplace safety
