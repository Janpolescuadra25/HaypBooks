**Morning Startup Routine:**

## Login & Access Issues

### Common Login Problems

**Problem:** "Invalid email or password"
- **Solution:** Reset password via "Forgot Password" link
- **Prevention:** Use password manager, enable two-factor authentication

**Problem:** "Account locked due to too many failed attempts"
- **Solution:** Wait 30 minutes or contact support for immediate unlock
- **Prevention:** Use correct credentials, avoid shared passwords

**Problem:** "Two-factor authentication code not received"
- **Solution:** Check spam folder, verify phone number, try backup codes
- **Prevention:** Keep phone number updated, save backup codes

### Access Permission Issues

**Problem:** "You don't have permission to access this feature"
- **Solution:** Contact administrator to update user permissions
- **Prevention:** Regularly review and update user roles

**Problem:** "Company file not accessible"
- **Solution:** Check internet connection, try different browser
- **Prevention:** Ensure stable internet, use supported browsers

## Transaction Problems

### Invoice Issues

**Problem:** Invoice not sending to customer
- **Check:** Customer email address is correct
- **Check:** Email settings are configured
- **Solution:** Update customer contact info, verify email settings

**Problem:** Invoice showing incorrect amounts
- **Check:** Tax calculations are correct
- **Check:** Item prices are current
- **Solution:** Update item prices, verify tax settings

### Payment Processing Issues

**Problem:** Payment not applying to invoice
- **Check:** Invoice number is correct
- **Check:** Customer name matches
- **Solution:** Use "Receive Payment" and manually apply to invoice

**Problem:** Duplicate payments recorded
- **Check:** Bank feed for duplicate transactions
- **Solution:** Delete duplicate, match to existing transaction

## Banking & Reconciliation Issues

### Bank Feed Problems

**Problem:** Transactions not downloading
- **Check:** Bank connection is active
- **Check:** Login credentials are correct
- **Solution:** Reconnect bank account, update credentials

**Problem:** Duplicate transactions in bank feed
- **Check:** For existing matches
- **Solution:** Use "Add to existing" or exclude duplicates

### Reconciliation Discrepancies

**Problem:** Account won't reconcile
- **Check:** Beginning balance matches bank statement
- **Check:** All transactions are categorized
- **Solution:** Verify opening balance, check for missing transactions

**Problem:** Reconciliation difference
- **Check:** Math calculations
- **Check:** Missing transactions
- **Solution:** Add adjusting entry for difference

## Haypbooks Terminology

### Basic Terms

**Chart of Accounts:** Complete list of all accounts in the general ledger
**General Ledger:** Master record of all financial transactions
**Double-Entry Accounting:** System where every transaction affects two accounts
**Debit:** Left side of accounting equation (increases assets/expenses)
**Credit:** Right side of accounting equation (increases liabilities/equity/revenue)

### Transaction Terms

**Invoice:** Bill sent to customers for goods or services
**Bill:** Invoice received from vendors for goods or services
**Receipt:** Proof of payment or transaction
**Journal Entry:** Manual accounting entry to record adjustments
**Reconciliation:** Process of matching Haypbooks records to bank statements

### Account Terms

**Asset:** Something owned by the business (cash, inventory, equipment)
**Liability:** Money owed by the business (loans, bills payable)
**Equity:** Owner's interest in the business
**Revenue:** Income from business operations
**Expense:** Cost of operating the business

---

## Accounting Terms

### Financial Statement Terms

**Balance Sheet:** Snapshot of financial position at a point in time
**Income Statement:** Report of revenues and expenses over a period
**Cash Flow Statement:** Report of cash inflows and outflows
**Retained Earnings:** Accumulated profits not distributed to owners
**Working Capital:** Current assets minus current liabilities

### Accounting Principles

**GAAP:** Generally Accepted Accounting Principles
**Accrual Basis:** Revenue and expenses recorded when earned/incurred
**Cash Basis:** Revenue and expenses recorded when cash changes hands
**Matching Principle:** Expenses matched with related revenues
**Conservatism:** When in doubt, record lower assets/liabilities

## Common Error Messages

### Login Errors

**Error:** "Invalid login credentials"
- **Cause:** Incorrect email/password
- **Solution:** Reset password, check caps lock

**Error:** "Account suspended"
- **Cause:** Billing or policy violation
- **Solution:** Contact support, resolve billing issues

### Transaction Errors

**Error:** "Insufficient permissions"
- **Cause:** User role restrictions
- **Solution:** Request elevated permissions

**Error:** "Duplicate transaction"
- **Cause:** Same transaction entered twice
- **Solution:** Delete duplicate, match to existing

### Banking Errors

**Error:** "Bank connection failed"
- **Cause:** Invalid credentials or bank issues
- **Solution:** Reconnect account, verify credentials

**Error:** "Reconciliation out of balance"
- **Cause:** Missing or incorrect transactions
- **Solution:** Find and correct discrepancies

## Banking Errors

### Connection Issues

- **Solution:** Check internet, try later

### Transaction Issues
