## Retail Industry Overview

### Retail Business Models

#### Brick-and-Mortar Retail
- **Traditional Stores**: Physical locations with in-store sales
- **Showrooms**: Display locations with online fulfillment
- **Pop-up Shops**: Temporary retail locations
- **Warehouse Stores**: Bulk retail with minimal in-store displays

#### E-commerce Models
- **Pure Online**: Digital-only retail operations
- **Marketplace Sellers**: Amazon, eBay, Etsy sellers
- **Dropshipping**: Third-party fulfillment models
- **Print-on-Demand**: Custom product manufacturing

#### Omnichannel Retail
- **Click-and-Collect**: Online ordering with in-store pickup
- **Buy Online, Return in Store**: Flexible return policies
- **In-Store Digital Integration**: Mobile payments and digital receipts
- **Unified Inventory**: Single inventory system across all channels

### Key Retail Metrics

#### Financial Metrics
- **Gross Margin**: (Sales - Cost of Goods Sold) / Sales
- **Inventory Turnover**: Cost of Goods Sold / Average Inventory
- **Sell-Through Rate**: Units Sold / Units Received
- **Stock-to-Sales Ratio**: Average Inventory / Average Monthly Sales

#### Operational Metrics
- **Conversion Rate**: Transactions / Store Traffic
- **Average Transaction Value**: Total Sales / Number of Transactions
- **Customer Acquisition Cost**: Marketing Spend / New Customers
- **Return Rate**: Returned Items / Total Items Sold

### Retail Accounting Challenges

#### Inventory Management
- **Cost Accounting**: Accurate cost tracking for inventory valuation
- **Stock Levels**: Real-time visibility across multiple locations
- **Shrinkage**: Loss prevention and inventory shrinkage tracking
- **Seasonal Variations**: Managing inventory for seasonal products

#### Revenue Recognition
- **Multi-Channel Sales**: Tracking sales across different platforms
- **Layaway Programs**: Deferred revenue recognition
- **Gift Cards**: Liability tracking and redemption
- **Loyalty Programs**: Accrued liability for future rewards

#### Cost Management
- **Supplier Terms**: Managing vendor payment terms and discounts
- **Shipping Costs**: Tracking and allocating shipping expenses
- **Store Operations**: Rent, utilities, and operational expenses
- **Marketing Costs**: Advertising and promotional expense tracking

- **Point-of-Sale Integration**: Immediate inventory reduction
- **Automatic Reordering**: Low stock alerts and reorder points
- **Cost of Goods Sold**: Real-time COGS calculation

### Inventory Valuation Methods

#### First-In, First-Out (FIFO)
- **Cost Flow**: Oldest inventory sold first
- **Inflation Benefit**: Higher profits during inflation
- **Tax Advantage**: Lower taxable income during inflation
- **Record Keeping**: Detailed cost layer tracking

#### Last-In, First-Out (LIFO)
- **Cost Flow**: Newest inventory sold first
- **Inflation Benefit**: Lower profits during inflation
- **Tax Advantage**: Higher taxable income during inflation
- **IRS Restrictions**: Limited availability for tax purposes

#### Average Cost
- **Cost Flow**: Average cost across all inventory
- **Simplicity**: Easier record keeping
- **Stability**: Smoother profit reporting
- **Tax Neutral**: No inflation-related tax advantages

#### Specific Identification
- **Cost Flow**: Actual cost tracking for each item
- **Accuracy**: Most accurate cost tracking
- **Complexity**: Requires detailed record keeping
- **Best For**: High-value, unique items

### Key Performance Indicators

#### Sales KPIs
- **Conversion Rate**: Website visitors to customers conversion
- **Average Order Value**: Average transaction amount
- **Customer Acquisition Cost**: Marketing cost per new customer
- **Customer Lifetime Value**: Total customer value over time

#### Inventory KPIs
- **Inventory Turnover**: Sales divided by average inventory
- **Sell-Through Rate**: Units sold divided by units received
- **Stock-to-Sales Ratio**: Inventory levels relative to sales
- **Out-of-Stock Rate**: Percentage of time items are unavailable

#### Operational KPIs
- **Gross Margin**: Sales minus cost of goods sold
- **Return Rate**: Returned items as percentage of total sales
- **Order Fulfillment Time**: Time from order to delivery
- **Customer Satisfaction**: Customer satisfaction survey results

#### Data Collection
- **Transaction Data**: Complete sales and transaction data
- **Customer Data**: Comprehensive customer profile information
